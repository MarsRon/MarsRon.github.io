About me

Hi, I'm MarsRon, also known as RontheDragon204 on Roblox. I'm an intermediate scripter on Roblox that is still learning to improve my scripting skills. I've started scripting since February of 2020 and have worked for many types of games, such as cafés, homestores, obbies and more. I haven't really touched on systems, including inventory systems, combat systems, weapon systems or vehicle systems, since I'm still just a learning scripter. Besides being a developer, I'm a nice friend to hangout with.
